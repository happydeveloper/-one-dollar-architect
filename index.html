AI Era

AI Can Write Code. It Cannot See the Whole System.

Today, AI can generate code, fix syntax errors, and even suggest architectures. I actively use AI tools as part of my workflow.

But real systems rarely fail because a piece of code is missing. They fail because of:

  • Wrong assumptions about users or traffic
  • Hidden operational constraints and edge cases
  • Business logic conflicts between teams
  • Data that behaves differently in the real world
  • Human decisions made under pressure
